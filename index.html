Coconut Shell Briquette 

Coconut Shell Briquette is compact block charcoal made from coconut shell charcoal. As a fuel, it's hotter and lasts longer than standard charcoal. Coconut briquettes are commonly used as a burner in shisha/hookah or barbecue.

Virgin Coconut Oil

Virgin coconut oil or VCO is a processed product made up of recent coconut that is processed at low temperatures or without going through heating, so the necessary content within the oil will be maintained.

Coconut Peat

Coco Fiber

Coco fiber is a natural fiber extracted from coconut husks. Coco fiber is widely used as a replacement for foam and other synthetic materials. For example, industrial raw materials for spring beds, mattresses, sofas, carpets, and ropes.
