The Black Gold From IndonesiaCoconut Shell Charcoal Briquette

Coconut Charcoal Briquette is a minimized square charcoal produced using coconut shell charcoal. Our charcoal briquette is made of granular coconut shell charcoal that is squashed charcoal and shaped utilizing a characteristic cover. As a fuel, it is more smoking and lasts longer than standard charcoal. It is appropriate for the family to utilize both indoors and outside. Likewise, it very well may be utilized in food enterprises, for a grill, oven fuel, metallurgy, and so on
