The Black Gold From IndonesiaCoconut Shell Charcoal Briquette

Coconut Charcoal Briquette is a minimized square charcoal produced using coconut shell charcoal. Our charcoal briquette is made of granular coconut shell charcoal that is squashed and shaped utilizing a characteristic cover. Coconut Shell Briquette is compact block charcoal made from coconut shell charcoal. As a fuel, it's hotter and lasts longer than standard charcoal. Coconut briquettes are commonly used as a burner in shisha/hookah or barbecue. It is appropriate for you to utilize it both indoors and outdoors. And also, it is very good for use in the food industry, grill fuel, oven fuel, metallurgy, and so on.
